Browser Extension

The Ledgerium AI Chrome extension captures your real browser workflows and converts them into structured, deterministic process documentation. It runs as a Chrome side panel — always accessible while you work.

Your starting point. Name your workflow, start a recording, and browse your recent captures — all in one view.

ⓘ Before your first recording, click Sync Settings and enter your API key from the Ledgerium AI dashboard (Settings → Extension).

Starting a Recording

After clicking Start Recording, the extension briefly prepares event listeners on your active tabs. This takes 1–2 seconds, then recording begins automatically.

Recording Your Workflow

Once recording starts, interact with any browser tab. The extension automatically detects clicks, form fills, navigation, and more — grouping raw browser events into clean, human-readable steps in real time.

Step cardsEach card shows a numbered step with a human-readable title, page URL, and step type

Pause, Stop & Review

Need a break or doing something you don't want captured? Pause suspends capture without losing any data. When you're done, click Stop & Review to process the recording.

PauseSuspend capture temporarily. All steps are preserved. Click Resume to continue.
Stop & ReviewEnd the recording. The engine finalizes steps, calculates confidence, and uploads the workflow.
DiscardAbandon the recording entirely and return to the home screen.

Error Handling

If something goes wrong (e.g., a tab is closed during recording), the extension shows a clear error message with context. Click Dismiss to return to the home screen and start a new recording.

ⓘ Data captured before the error is preserved when possible. You can start a new recording immediately.

Tips & Best Practices

Before Recording

  1. Use a descriptive name — "Process vendor invoice in SAP" is better than "Test"
  2. Close unnecessary tabs to reduce noise
  3. Know the start and end points of your workflow

During Recording

  1. Work at a natural pace — fast or slow is fine
  2. Use Pause for interruptions or side tasks
  3. Keep the side panel open for best results

After Recording

  1. Review the generated SOP for accuracy
  2. Record the same process multiple times for higher confidence
  3. Share workflows with your team from the dashboard

Getting Started

Get up and running in under 5 minutes:

  1. Sign up for a free Ledgerium AI account at ledgerium.ai/signup
  2. Install the extension from the Chrome Web Store and open the Ledgerium AI side panel
  3. Generate an API key from your Account page and paste it into the extension's Sync Settings
  4. Record a workflow — name it, click Start, perform your process, then Stop & Review
  5. View your results on the dashboard — process map, SOP, insights, and AI analysis are generated automatically
