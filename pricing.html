FAQ

What is a "recorder" vs. a "seat"?
A recorder is someone who captures workflows using the Chrome extension. A seat is anyone on your team who can view, share, and act on the generated SOPs, process maps, and intelligence reports. Most teams have 1-3 recorders and many more viewers.
What is the intelligence layer?
The intelligence layer includes bottleneck detection, friction analysis, rework pattern identification, variant path analysis, automation opportunity scoring, and process health scores. It turns raw workflow recordings into actionable process improvement insights. Available on Team plans and above.
Can I try before I buy?
Yes. The Free plan gives you 5 recordings per month with full SOP and process map output. No credit card required. Upgrade when you need more recordings, clean exports, or the intelligence layer.
How does annual billing work?
Annual billing saves 17% compared to monthly (roughly 2 months free). You can switch between monthly and annual at any time from your account settings.
Do you store my data?
Workflow recordings are processed securely and stored in your workspace. Enterprise plans support custom retention policies and on-premise deployment options. All processing is deterministic and auditable.
Can we run this behind the firewall?
Enterprise plans can support private deployments depending on requirements. Contact sales for details.
