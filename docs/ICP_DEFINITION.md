## Primary ICP: Operations Team Lead

### Who they are
- Title: Operations Manager, Process Lead, Team Lead, Sr. Analyst
- Team size: 5-50 people they support
- Company size: 50-5,000 employees
- Industry: Financial services, insurance, healthcare admin, SaaS operations, shared services

### What they do
- Maintain SOPs for browser-based internal tools (ERP, CRM, ticketing, HR systems)
- Onboard new team members who need to learn complex multi-system workflows
- Respond to audit requests for process documentation
- Identify where processes break down or vary between team members

### Their pain today
- SOPs go stale within weeks of being written
- Writing SOPs takes hours of interviews and manual documentation
- They know the documented process doesn't match reality but can't prove it
- New hires follow the SOP and make errors because it's incomplete
- Process improvement projects start with weeks of "discovery" (workshops, shadowing)

### Why Ledgerium solves it
- Records the actual workflow (not what someone remembers)
- Generates an SOP automatically (saves hours of documentation work)
- Shows step timing, confidence, and system context (reveals where the process is fragile)
- Evidence-linked output (useful for audit and compliance conversations)

### Activation signal
"I recorded a workflow and the SOP output was good enough to share with my team."

### Expansion path
1. One team lead records their key workflows → validates output quality
2. They share SOPs with team members → validates usefulness
3. Other team leads in the org adopt → validates team distribution
4. Compliance/risk team notices → validates the evidence angle

## What this means for the beta

1. **Recruit beta users who match this ICP** — ops team leads who maintain SOPs for browser workflows
2. **Sample workflow should reflect their context** — an operations workflow (expense report, purchase order, ticket creation), not a generic "demo"
3. **Demo page should show their workflow** — screenshots of a real ops workflow being recorded and the resulting SOP
4. **Success = they share the SOP with a colleague** — not just that they viewed it
