# Ledgerium AI — Positioning Decision

**Status:** DECISION REQUIRED before beta launch
**Date:** 2026-04-12

---

## The Decision

Ledgerium must choose its primary positioning axis for launch. The current messaging hedges between two valid but incompatible positions:

### Option A: Process Documentation Leader
**Message:** "Record real workflows. Get SOPs instantly."
**Competes with:** Scribe, Tango, manual documentation
**Buyer:** Ops team leads, trainers, onboarding managers
**Advantage:** Larger market, faster feedback loops, self-serve motion
**Risk:** Invites screenshot comparison; Scribe has massive lead
**What to add:** Optional screenshot capture, Notion/Confluence export

### Option B: Process Evidence Platform
**Message:** "Auditable process evidence from real work."
**Competes with:** Manual attestation, task mining tools (UiPath, Celonis)
**Buyer:** Compliance analysts, risk teams, quality managers
**Advantage:** No direct competitor; architecture perfectly aligned; budget exists
**Risk:** Narrower market, longer sales cycle, requires enterprise readiness
**What to add:** Audit trail exports, compliance report templates

---

## Recommendation

**Lead with Option A for beta. Position for Option B as the expansion wedge.**

Rationale:
- Option A users can self-serve and provide fast activation data
- The SOP output is the most tangible, immediate-value deliverable
- Option B requires enterprise features (SSO, team workspaces) that don't exist yet
- Once the core loop is validated with Option A users, the compliance narrative becomes credible because you have proof the product works

**Concrete changes needed:**

1. **Homepage hero:** Keep "Your SOP says 5 steps. Your team takes 17."
2. **Primary persona:** "Operations team leads who maintain SOPs for browser-based workflows"
3. **Demo example:** Show a real ops workflow (expense report, purchase order, ticket creation)
4. **Secondary positioning:** "Built for evidence. Ready for compliance." as a trust section
5. **Remove or de-emphasize:** "AI-fluid workflow layer" language until Phase 5

---

## One-Line Positioning Statement

**"Ledgerium records how browser work actually happens and generates SOPs, process maps, and workflow reports automatically — with every step traced to observed evidence."**

**Anti-positioning:** "We are not a screen recorder, not an AI document generator, and not a surveillance tool."
