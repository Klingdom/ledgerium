Deterministic core, optional AI

Use AI for suggestions and language—but keep canonical outputs deterministic.

Key points

  • Keep raw evidence immutable.
  • Generate the map/SOP from rules first.
  • Layer AI as an assistive pass (labeled, reversible).
  • Never allow AI to silently rewrite evidence.
