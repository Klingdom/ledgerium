The trust contract

Ledgerium AI earns trust by staying legible: what we capture, why we capture it, how it’s stored, and how outputs are derived.

1) Capture only what’s needed

  • Default to minimal interaction telemetry
  • Redact sensitive values by rule
  • Allow “no-capture zones” (domains/pages)

2) Preserve raw truth

  • Raw events are immutable
  • Derived steps stored separately
  • Exports include both layers

3) Deterministic derivation

  • Published rules
  • Versioned rulesets
  • Same input → same output

4) AI is optional (never canonical)

  • AI suggestions are labeled and reversible
  • No silent edits to evidence
  • Human review is first-class
Plain-language summary: You should always be able to answer, “Why does the SOP say that?” with a click to the evidence.
