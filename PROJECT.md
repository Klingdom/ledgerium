# Project Definition

## Product type
A software and SaaS product development system capable of building and improving:
- marketing websites
- web applications
- internal tools
- APIs and backend services
- analytics and operational dashboards
- onboarding and growth systems

## Primary goals
- Ship valuable product increments quickly
- Maintain production-quality engineering standards
- Learn rapidly through measured experiments
- Build reusable systems and patterns
- Improve customer acquisition, activation, retention, and monetization

## Target users
- end users of the SaaS product
- administrators and operators
- internal product, support, and engineering teams
- buyers evaluating the product

## Product pillars
- usability
- reliability
- speed
- security
- clarity
- measurable business value

## What success looks like
- users can understand the product quickly
- users can complete core workflows with low friction
- engineering can ship safely and frequently
- infrastructure is stable and observable
- product decisions are grounded in evidence
- growth efforts are measurable and iterative
